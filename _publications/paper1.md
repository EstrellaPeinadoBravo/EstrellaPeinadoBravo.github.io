The purpose of this study is to validate the Oxford Scenario Planning Approach (OSPA) from an emerging economy perspective. To do so, we use in-depth interviews to key business leadership associations (local and foreign), discovering underlying perceptions, which sheds insights into future situations. 

[Forthcoming paper at Journal of Business (Universidad del Pacífico)](https://revistas.up.edu.pe/index.php/business)
