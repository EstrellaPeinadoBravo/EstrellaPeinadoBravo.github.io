The objective of the study is to contribute to the efforts of the export promotion policies of the La Libertad region (Peru) and thus, improve the export performance of firms. To meet this aim, this article advances an alternative methodology to identify and evaluate potential export opportunities for the exportable supply of La Libertad region in Peru.
