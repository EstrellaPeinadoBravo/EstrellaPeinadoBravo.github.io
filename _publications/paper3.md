This document seeks to explore the strategic relevance of the port of Chancay at the sub-national level. The document consists of two sections.  The first one builds upon a theoretical framework on city-ports to understand the current situation of the port of Chancay in Peru in terms of its geopolitical environment and relevance at the international and subnational levels. The second one presents four lines of research that need to be explored in greater detail.
